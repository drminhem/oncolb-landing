User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: February 26, 2026

If you want OncoLB to delete personal data associated with your use of our messaging channels, submit a deletion request by email.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to m.minhem1@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include the phone number or email address associated with your communication.

Verification and Processing

  • We may request information to verify ownership of the data before deletion.
  • After verification, we will delete or anonymize eligible data from operational systems.
  • Certain records may be retained where required by law or regulatory obligations.

Timeline

Verified deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
